A brief note on college rivalries

As of the 12th of June 2016, Hertford College JCR have passed a motion declaring Hilda's College their college rivals.

We have resolved:

  • To officially announce St. Hilda’s as our college rival
  • To "declare war" on St. Hilda’s college
  • To mandate the JCR president to inform the Hilda’s president of resolves 1 and 2
  • To stock up on arms such as water balloons and nerf guns in case of an actual battle
  • To mandate HCMS to create an anti-Hilda’s chant (preferably with optional degrees of profanity)
