Hertford JCR Structure

The JCR holds three evening meetings each term, which are open to all members, and often have a lot of pizza. At these meetings, a summary of what has been happening in the JCR is given, all JCR members can present motions (if sent to the Secretary beforehand), and there may be hustings for positions. The various positions that can be held are listed below. Positions are usually held for a year.

The Executive Committee consists of the following officers:

  • President
  • Treasurer (and VP)
  • Secretary (and VP)
  • Food & Housing Officer
  • Welfare Officers
  • Sports Officer
  • Access Officer
  • Academic Affairs Officer
  • IT Officer

The Committee of Representatives consists of the following reps:

  • Arts Rep
  • Bar Rep
  • BME Rep
  • Charities Rep(s)
  • Disability Rep
  • Equal Opportunities Reps
  • Environment and Ethics Rep
  • International Students’ Rep
  • LGBTQ Rep(s)
  • OUSU Rep
  • Women’s Rep

There are also a number of smaller committees:

  • Ball Committee
  • Entz Committee
  • Freshers Reception Committee
  • Independent Committee of Auditors
  • Simpkins Magazine Committee
  • Yearbook Committee
